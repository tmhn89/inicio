1

FOR STUDENTS

Provide Swedish children between the ages of 13-15 with practical, hands-on experiences that help them explore their interests, discover their passion, and find the right high school program to apply to.


2

FOR SCHOOLS

We team up with educators to support students with their decision-making towards high school, we provide the opportunities for everyone to find their passion and develop their talents as we introduce the school itself to a different way of learning.

3

FOR BUSINESSES

We provide the industry with the opportunity to partner with us and take part in the educational project that inspires students to find and pursue their passion; together, we will impact the lives of young talents and their communities.
